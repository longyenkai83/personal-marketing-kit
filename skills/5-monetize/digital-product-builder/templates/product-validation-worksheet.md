## 📝 STEP 9 — SALES PAGE COPY (Sam Cart)

### Page structure
1. **Hero**: Product name + tagline + mockup video
2. **3-Pillar headline**: combining curiosity + specific + unbelievable
3. **Value bullets** (4-6)
4. **Social proof**: testimonial OR Use Proof popup
5. **What's inside**: detailed breakdown
6. **ROI angle**: how they make money back / save / identity shift
7. **Pricing + scarcity** (optional)
8. **FAQ** (5-7 common questions)
9. **CTA button** repeated 3-5 times
10. **P.S.** — last objection handling

### Copy checklist
- [ ] Headline incorporates 3 pillars
- [ ] First 3-5 bullets specific (no fluff)
- [ ] ROI clearly articulated
- [ ] Refund policy clear (build trust)
- [ ] CTA button color contrasts page
- [ ] Mobile-readable (test on phone)

## 🎯 STEP 12 — POST-LAUNCH OPTIMIZATION

### Sau 30 ngày, audit:

1. **Email với highest open rate** → replicate pattern
2. **Page với highest exit rate** → fix friction point
3. **Top objection từ DM/replies** → add to FAQ
4. **Top question audience asks** → next product idea

### Iterate cycles
- Week 1-4: Test pricing
- Week 5-8: Test messaging (headlines, bullets)
- Week 9-12: Test mockup variants
- Week 13+: Scale winning version
