## 💰 PRICING STRATEGY

### Sweet Spot: $50-100 for First Product

> *"For most people creating digital products — courses, PDFs, audio bundles — $50 to $100 is the perfect price range to start."*
> — Maria

### Why $50-100 Sweet Spot?

| Price | Pros | Cons |
|-------|------|------|
| <$20 | Easy first sale | No-perceived-value, audience think "junk" |
| $20-50 | Good for first 5 sales | Feels too cheap for main product |
| **$50-100** ⭐ | **Sweet spot** | Best volume × revenue balance |
| $150-300 | Premium feel | Hard to viral, slow growth |
| >$500 | High margin per sale | Niche audience only, no virality |

### VN Adaptation (USD → VND)

- $50 ≈ **1.2tr** ✅
- $97 ≈ **2.3tr** ✅ (sweet spot Maria most charges)
- $300 ≈ **7tr** (high tier when authority strong)
- $1 (early bird) ≈ **25k**
- $37 (entry) ≈ **890k**

### Recommended VN price ladder

| Stage | Price VND | When |
|-------|-----------|------|
| First 5 sales | 500k-1tr | Get testimonials |
| Steady state | 1.2-2.5tr | Sweet spot |
| Bundle/high tier | 5-7tr | After 100+ sales, authority strong |

## 🧪 TEST RULE — Maria's Exact Method

### The 90-Day Diagnostic

```
IF (90 days no sales) {
  Run $1 flash sale 2 days
  
  IF (still no sales at $1) {
    Problem = MARKETING/MESSAGING (NOT price)
    → Re-do: messaging, niche, mockup, sales page
  } ELSE {
    Problem = PRICE
    → Either lower price OR rework value perception
  }
}
```

### Why $1 test?

- $1 = removes price objection completely
- If still no buyers → product/messaging is the problem
- If buyers come → price was the blocker
- Gives clear diagnostic in 48h

### Implementation

1. Set product to $1 for exactly 2 days
2. Send 1 broadcast email to list
3. Post 1 IG/FB post about flash sale
4. Track sales count
5. Decision tree above

## 📈 VOLUME STRATEGY (Maria's Mindset)

> *"Tôi thích ý tưởng 100 chiến dịch làm việc 24/7 cho tôi."*
> — Maria

### Why volume > high-price?

| Approach | Math | Audience effect |
|----------|------|-----------------|
| **High price low volume** | $300 × 10 sales = $3000 | Small list, slow growth |
| **Sweet spot high volume** | $97 × 50 sales = $4850 | Big list, fast growth, recurring sales future |

→ Sweet spot **WINS revenue** + **WINS list growth** + **WINS social proof**.

### Anti-pattern Maria warns

❌ "Tôi xứng đáng charge $300 vì sản phẩm có giá trị"
→ Sản phẩm có thể có giá trị, NHƯNG:
- Audience mới không trust để pay $300
- Volume thấp → momentum chết
- Niche audience only → không scale

✅ Charge sweet spot, build volume, raise price after 100+ sales when authority strong.

### Maria's evolution

- 4 năm bán sản phẩm số = $7M total
- Started: $20-50 products
- Sweet spot: $97-197 (current main range)
- High tier: $300-500 (for premium bundles, after years of authority)
- $7M / 4 years = ~$2M/year average = mostly volume play

## 💸 PRICE LADDER (Tripwire → Mid → High)

### Maria's exact ladder

| Tier | Price USD | Price VND | Purpose |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| **Tripwire** | $1-7 | 25k-170k | Convert visitor → buyer (xem `tripwire-builder` skill) |
| **Entry** | $27-37 | 650k-890k | Mini-course, low commitment |
| **Mid** ⭐ | **$97** | **2.3tr** | **Sweet spot main product** |
| **High** | $197-300 | 4.7-7tr | Bundle, comprehensive course |
| **Premium** | $497+ | 12tr+ | Coaching cohort, 1-1, limited |

### Workflow

1. **First product**: aim for **Mid tier ($97 / 2.3tr)**
2. **After 30 sales**: build Entry tier ($37) feeding into Mid
3. **After 100 sales**: build Tripwire ($1) feeding into Entry → Mid
4. **After 200 sales**: build High tier ($197) for upsell

→ Build **bottom-up** (start mid, add tripwire later) — NOT top-down.

## 🆓 BOOTSTRAP VERSION (Free start)

For first 5-10 sales before paying for tools:

| Need | Free option | Trade-off |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| Course host | **Google Drive** | Looks unprofessional, kills conversion 30-50% |
| Checkout | **Venmo / PayPal direct link** | Manual process, lose 3am buyers, no automation |
| Email | **MailerLite free tier** (1k contacts) | Limited automation, deliverability OK not great |
| Payment | Manual transfer | Friction high |

### When to upgrade from bootstrap?

- After **5 paid sales** = product validated
- After **$300 revenue** = self-pays Sam Cart for 5 months
- After **$500 revenue** = self-pays both Sam Cart + Kajabi

→ Don't upgrade until product validated. Don't STAY bootstrap once validated (you're losing $).

## 🛒 SAM CART CONVERSION HACKS

### Maria's 3 must-have features

1. **Mockup video** at top of checkout
   - Big, prominent
   - Show ALL bundled items
   - Quantity emphasis ("100+ templates")

2. **Use Proof popup**
   - Real-time "X just bought" notification
   - Creates FOMO + social proof
   - Easy to install (Use Proof = third-party)

3. **Add billing info to form**
   - Reduces card declines (banks see "matching billing" → trust higher)
   - Increases conversion 5-10%

### Other Sam Cart features Maria uses

- Order bump (add-on at checkout — vd: "Add Hook Writer for $7")
- One-click upsell (post-purchase, frictionless extra sale)
- Trial / payment plans (for premium tier $300+)

## 🚫 Common Pricing Mistakes

### 1. Charge premium price out of gate
❌ Beginner price $300 because "I'm worth it"
→ No sales because no authority yet
✅ Start sweet spot, raise after authority

### 2. Race to bottom
❌ Charge $7 forever to "sell more"
→ Audience perceive as junk, hurt brand
✅ $50-100 is psychological sweet spot

### 3. Don't test
❌ Set price once, never iterate
✅ A/B test prices for 30-day periods, optimize

### 4. Forget price ladder
❌ Single price point, no upsell/downsell
✅ Build ladder (tripwire → entry → mid → high)

### 5. Discount instead of bonuses
❌ "30% OFF" → audience trains to wait for discount
✅ Add bonus value at full price (template, bonus module, 1-1 review)

### 6. Wrong currency
❌ VN audience seeing $97 USD → confused, friction
✅ Show VND for VN, USD for international
