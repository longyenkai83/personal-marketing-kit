## Triết lý cốt lõi

> **Tripwire = sản phẩm $1-7 chuyển VISITOR thành BUYER ngay lập tức.**
> 
> Một khi audience đã pay $1, psychology shift: từ "skeptical" → "customer" → 5-10x dễ buy entry/mid/high tier sau.

## Why Tripwire works (psychology)

### Conversion math

| Stage | Without tripwire | With tripwire |
|-------|------------------|---------------|
| Visitor → Lead | 5% (download free) | 5% (same) |
| Lead → First buyer | 1% (try $97 cold) | **15-25%** ($1 tripwire) |
| First buyer → Mid buyer | 5% (cold to $97) | **20-40%** (already trusted) |
| **End-to-end** | 0.05% | **0.6-1.0%** (10-20x lift) |

→ Tripwire = **multiplier funnel**.

### Psychology at play

1. **Foot in the door** — small commitment → larger commitments easier
2. **Loss aversion** — already paid $1 → don't want to "waste" it → engage with content
3. **Identity shift** — "buyer of [Brand]" identity → next purchase frictionless
4. **Filter quality leads** — paying $1 = serious vs free downloaders (junk)

## 🎯 TRIPWIRE TYPES (4 main)

### Type 1: $1 Worksheet/Template Pack

**Maria's Welcome Coupon = này = kiếm $50k/month**

- Price: $1-7 (25k-170k VND)
- Format: Editable PDF, Canva template, Google Sheet tracker
- Promise: 1 quick-win in <30 min

✅ Examples Maria-style:
- "12-Month Business Gameplan Worksheets — $1"
- "100 Subject Line Swipes — $7"
- "30-Day Content Calendar Template — $5"

✅ VN adaptation:
- "Worksheet 5 bước đóng gói chuyên môn — 25k"
- "Template 30 caption Reels — 99k"
- "Lịch content 30 ngày editable — 49k"

### Type 2: Mini-course $7-27

- Format: 3-5 short videos (5-10 min each)
- Promise: Specific transformation in 1 area
- Typically upsell to main course

✅ Examples:
- "5-Day Email List Sprint — $7"
- "How To Sell Your First Digital Product — $27"

### Type 3: 7-Day Email Course $0-27

> **Maria's 7-Day Email Course Automation** = nurture nurture nurture then scoop up cash

- Format: Daily email for 7 days with lessons
- Can be free (lead magnet) OR paid tripwire ($7-27)
- Promise: Mastery of 1 micro-skill in 1 week

✅ Examples:
- "7-Day Inbox Profit Sprint — Free"
- "7-Day Reels Camera Confidence — $17"

### Type 4: Audio Quick Tips $1-7

- Format: 5-10 audio files (5-15 min each)
- Promise: Listen on commute, learn fast
- Lower production cost

✅ Examples:
- "10 Audio Lessons: Mom-preneur Mindset — $7"
- "Daily Pep Talk Audio Pack — $5"

## 🔄 WELCOME COUPON FLOW (Maria's $50k/month playbook)

### Trigger
User opt-in (download lead magnet, signup email list).

### Sequence

```
TRIGGER (opt-in)
  ↓
EMAIL 1 (immediate): Deliver lead magnet + Welcome Coupon $1 offer
  ↓
EMAIL 2 (day 1): Story + soft mention coupon
  ↓
EMAIL 3 (day 3): "Coupon expires in 24h" reminder
  ↓
EMAIL 4 (day 4): "Last chance — coupon expires tonight"
  ↓
[After purchase OR coupon expires]
  ↓
EMAIL 5+: Continue normal welcome sequence + upsell mid-tier
```

### Email 1 details (CRITICAL — first impression)

```
Subject: "[Tên freebie] đây + 1 thứ đặc biệt"

Body:
Chào [first_name],

Đây là [tên freebie] em hứa gửi:
👉 [DOWNLOAD LINK]

Vì chị đã trust em đăng ký nhận email, em muốn tặng chị 1 deal đặc biệt:

[TRIPWIRE PRODUCT] — chỉ $1 hôm nay (giá thường $27).

[Brief description — 2-3 bullets value]

Coupon hết hạn sau 48h.

👉 [GET IT FOR $1 LINK]

Reply lại nếu chị có question.

— Maria 🤍
```

### Email 3-4 details (urgency)

```
Subject: "Coupon $1 sắp hết — 24h cuối"

Body:
Chào [first_name],

Nhanh — coupon $1 cho [tripwire product] hết hạn tối nay.

Reminder why:
[1-2 lý do mạnh tại sao đáng]

👉 [LINK $1 deal]

### Once buyer purchases $1 tripwire:

```
PURCHASE CONFIRMATION
  ↓

  ↓ Show ONE-CLICK UPSELL
  "Add [Mid-tier product] for $XX more (vs $97 separately)"
  ↓

  ↓

  ↓
EMAIL 3 (day 3): Story + mention mid-tier
  ↓

### Upsell ratios Maria sees

- 30-40% take **one-click upsell** at thank-you page
- 5-10% take **mid-tier** within 7 days post-tripwire
- 15-25% take **mid-tier** within 30 days post-tripwire

→ **Average AOV** từ tripwire buyer: $30-80 (vs $1 nominal price).

## 🛠 TECH SETUP

### Required tools

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Sam Cart** | Tripwire checkout page + one-click upsell |
| **Active Campaign** | Email automation triggered by purchase |
| **Manychat** (optional) | Free list-building before tripwire |
| **Kajabi** | Host tripwire content |

### Setup steps

1. **Create tripwire product** in Kajabi (or Google Drive nếu bootstrap)
2. **Build Sam Cart checkout page** với:
   - Mockup Maria-style
   - Use Proof popup
   - One-click upsell to mid-tier
3. **Setup Active Campaign automation**:
   - Trigger: "User opted in to [list]"
   - Action: Send Email 1 with coupon link
   - Wait 2 days
   - If NOT purchased → Send Email 3 (urgency)
   - Wait 1 day
   - If NOT purchased → Send Email 4 (last chance)
   - If PURCHASED → Move to upsell sequence
4. **Setup post-purchase automation**:
   - Trigger: "Purchased tripwire"
   - Send 5-7 emails over 7 days driving to mid-tier
5. **Test end-to-end** (yourself)

- Track tripwire conversion %

- A/B test pricing ($1 vs $7) and messaging

## Failure modes

- **Lead → Tripwire conversion <10%** → Coupon offer not strong enough OR tripwire too expensive. Lower to $1.
- **Tripwire → Mid-tier <10%** → Bridge weak. Tripwire should solve micro-version of mid-tier's problem.
- **High refund rate on $1** → Product over-promise. Audit deliverable.
- **Audience confused at coupon offer** → Email 1 unclear. Simplify.
- **No urgency response** → Coupon expiry not credible. Use real time-limit.
