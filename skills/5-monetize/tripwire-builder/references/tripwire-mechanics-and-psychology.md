## 🧠 PSYCHOLOGY — Tại sao $1 lại work?

### 1. FOOT-IN-THE-DOOR Principle

> Small commitment makes larger commitment easier.

Once audience pay $1:
- Identity shift: "skeptical" → "customer"
- Mental commitment: invested in your world
- Next purchase: less friction

**Studies show**: Users who pay $1 are **5-10x more likely** to buy higher-tier later vs free downloaders.

### 2. LOSS AVERSION

> People feel loss 2x more than gain.

Once paid $1:
- "I don't want to waste $1" → use product
- Use product → see value → buy more

→ Free downloads sit unopened. $1 purchases get used.

### 3. IDENTITY SHIFT

Free download = "tôi đang exploring"
Paid $1 = "tôi là customer của brand này"

Identity shift → behavior aligned with identity.

### 4. FILTER QUALITY

- Free leads: 90% junk (curiosity click), 10% serious
- $1 buyers: 100% serious (willing to pay)

→ $1 audience = **WAY higher LTV** for nurture sequences.

### 5. CHEAP ANCHOR

Show $27 crossed out → $1 displayed → audience feel "winning $26".

Even though $1 is the actual price, anchoring makes deal feel premium.

## 💎 4 TRIPWIRE TYPES — Strategic comparison

### Type 1: Worksheet/Template Pack ($1)

**Best for**: Lowest production effort, highest volume.

**Examples**:
- Maria's "12-Month Business Gameplan Worksheets"
- "30 IG Caption Templates"
- "Email Subject Line Swipe File"

**Pros**:
- 1-2 hours to create in Canva
- Easy to update/iterate
- Universal appeal (templates always wanted)

**Cons**:
- Lower perceived value (just PDF)
- Lower upsell rate to mid-tier

**Conversion math**:
- Lead → $1 worksheet: 15-20%
- $1 worksheet → mid-tier (7d): 10-15%

### Type 2: Mini-Course ($7-27)

**Best for**: Tighter bridge to mid-tier, higher intent.

**Examples**:
- "5-Day Email List Sprint" $7
- "How To Sell Your First Digital Product" $27

**Pros**:
- Higher perceived value
- Stronger nurture (audience watch videos)
- Higher upsell to mid-tier

**Cons**:
- Days to create (record videos)
- Need editing
- Lower conversion vs $1

**Conversion math**:
- Lead → $7-27 mini-course: 5-10%
- Mini-course → mid-tier (7d): 20-30%

### Type 3: 7-Day Email Course ($0-27)

**Best for**: Long nurture, complex mid-tier sale.

**Examples**:
- "7-Day Inbox Profit Sprint" Free
- "7-Day Reels Camera Confidence" $17

**Pros**:
- Email already built (just sequence)
- Daily touch builds relationship
- Day 7 pitch with high context

**Cons**:
- 7-day delay before mid-tier pitch
- Audience may forget by day 7
- Lower immediate revenue

**Conversion math** (free version):
- Lead → 7-day course: 50-70% (if mandatory opt-in)
- 7-day course → mid-tier: 5-15%

### Type 4: Audio Quick Tips ($1-7)

**Best for**: Mobile audience, on-the-go niche.

**Examples**:
- "10 Audio Lessons: Mom-preneur Mindset" $7
- "Daily Pep Talk Pack" $5

**Pros**:
- Easy to create (phone voice memo)
- Listen anywhere
- Differentiation from PDF/video market

**Cons**:
- Lower demand than templates
- Hard to scan/skim (audience commit time to listen)

## 🎨 DESIGN RULES — Maria's principles

### Rule 1: SUPER SPECIFIC outcome

❌ Generic: "Improve your business"
✅ Specific: "Get your first 10 online customers in 30 days"

### Rule 2: QUICK WIN < 30 minutes

> Audience just paid $1 → expect IMMEDIATE value.

Tripwire phải deliver:
- Outcome trong < 30 phút
- Actionable step-by-step
- No "you need 7 days to see results"

### Rule 3: BRIDGE to mid-tier logical

Tripwire = micro-version of mid-tier's problem.

✅ Logical bridge:
- Tripwire: "5 IG Caption Templates" → Mid-tier: "Full IG Marketing Course"
- Tripwire: "$1 Email Subject Line Swipes" → Mid-tier: "Inbox to Income $97 course"

❌ Disconnect:
- Tripwire: "Yoga Poses" → Mid-tier: "Email Marketing"
- → Audience confused, no upsell take rate

### Rule 4: HIGH PERCEIVED VALUE for $1

Even at $1, audience expect quality.

- Polished PDF (not raw notes)
- Branded design (not Word doc)
- Multiple files included (not just 1)
- "Bonus" element added

### Rule 5: NUMBER prominent

Like main mockup rule — show "+":
- "100+ Templates"
- "30 Worksheets"
- "12 Modules"

Quantity emphasis = perceived high value.

## 💸 ECONOMICS — Tripwire profit math

### Single tripwire sale economics

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Tripwire sale | $1 |
| Sam Cart fee (3%) | -$0.03 |
| Stripe fee (2.9% + $0.30) | -$0.33 |
| Net per sale | **$0.64** |

→ $1 tripwire = ~**$0.64 net**.

### But true value = LIFETIME VALUE

Tripwire buyers (with proper upsell flow):
- 30% take one-click upsell ($20) → +$6 average
- 20% take mid-tier within 7d ($97) → +$19 average
- 5% take high-tier within 30d ($297) → +$15 average

**Average tripwire buyer LTV**: ~$40-60 first 30 days.

→ Tripwire is **NOT** about $1. It's about **converting visitor to customer at scale**.

### Acquisition math

If you spend $5 cost-per-lead via ads:
- Without tripwire: 1% lead → mid-tier sale ($97) = $97 - $5×100 = -$403 LOSS per 100 leads
- With tripwire: 15% lead → tripwire ($1), 20% tripwire → mid-tier ($97) = ~$300 - $500 = -$200 LOSS

→ Hmm, both lose? But:
- Tripwire revenue **buys you back** → ads profitable faster
- Tripwire buyers convert higher in long-term sequences (60-day, 90-day)
- True LTV after 90 days: $80-120/buyer

→ Tripwire **transforms** ads from "loss" to "profit machine" once long-term LTV kicks in.

## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES

### 1. Tripwire price too high ($27+)

Loses "no-brainer" magic. $1-7 sweet spot for Maria-style.

### 2. No urgency on coupon

If coupon "always available" → audience procrastinate forever → no buy.

✅ 48-72h hard expiry.

### 3. Tripwire → Mid-tier disconnect

If tripwire about A, mid-tier about B, audience confused.

✅ Tripwire = appetizer of mid-tier meal.

### 4. Hard sell mid-tier in tripwire emails

Audience just paid $1 — DON'T immediately push $97. Build trust first.

✅ Days 1-3 nurture, day 4-7 gradual mid-tier intro.

### 5. Tripwire over-promises, under-delivers

If $1 product disappoints, audience NEVER buys mid-tier.

✅ $1 product should feel like "wow, $27 value at $1" — over-deliver.

### 6. No one-click upsell at thank-you page

Lose 30-40% of revenue opportunity.

✅ Sam Cart one-click upsell IMMEDIATELY after purchase.

### 7. Same email frequency post-purchase as before

Buyer expectations different. Need different sequence.

✅ Build SEPARATE post-purchase automation in Active Campaign.
