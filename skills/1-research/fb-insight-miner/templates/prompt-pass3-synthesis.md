# Pass 3 — Synthesis (Hidden patterns)

Take the outputs of Pass 1 (breadth) and Pass 2 (depth). Do NOT repeat them. Find NEW insight that only emerges when you cross-examine everything together.

## 3.1 Cross-theme patterns

- **Co-occurrence** — which dimensions frequently appear together in the same comment?
  - E.g., "Pain + Objection" → they hurt but still won't buy → trust issue?
  - E.g., "Desire + Competitor mention" → they want X and are considering [brand Y] → competitive threat
- **Contradictions** — do they ASK for one thing but COMPLAIN about it when given?
  - E.g., ask for "free content" but complain content is "too surface level"
- **Bridge metaphors** — metaphors that appear across multiple dimensions
  - E.g., "ngộp thở" used for both pain AND for desire (want space)

## 3.2 Silent signals

Use `references/silent-signals-guide.md`. Review the POST metadata (not comment content):

### 3.2.a High-view low-comment posts
List posts where view/comment ratio > 50:1.
→ **Sensitive topics** — gold for DM marketing & private group offers.

### 3.2.b High-reaction low-comment posts
List posts with many likes/loves but few comments.
→ **Weak CTA** — the hook works, prompt doesn't. Replicate hook, fix CTA.

### 3.2.c Unanswered customer questions
Search comments: `ends with "?"` AND `has no admin reply`.
→ **Offer gap OR content gap** — list top 10 unanswered questions. Each is a product/content idea.

### 3.2.d High-reply-count comments (heated threads)
Top 10 comments with most replies. What sparked debate?
→ Controversial = engagement = content goldmine.

## 3.3 Temporal drift

Split comments into 3 equal time buckets (early / mid / recent). For each:
- Top 3 themes (from the 9 dimensions)
- Vocabulary shift — any new slang? new pain points?
- Trend direction — emerging / stable / declining

**Synthesis question:** What pain is FADING? What pain is RISING?
- Fading pain → stop making content about it
- Rising pain → next content series

## 3.4 Unmet expectations

- What do customers REPEATEDLY ask for that the page does NOT deliver?
- What do they EXPECT based on the page's brand promise but complain is missing?
- Rank by frequency.

## 3.5 Persona refinement — THE ONE PARAGRAPH

Combine all signals into a single 1-paragraph persona:

> "[Name] is a [age]-year-old [profession/life-stage] in [location], who spends her evenings [context]. Her primary pain is [X]. Her secret desire is [Y]. She's tried [list] and been burned by [common objection]. She responds best to [media type] when it feels like [emotional tone]. In her own words, her problem sounds like: '[verbatim quote]'. What she really wants, but can't articulate, is [deeper desire]."
